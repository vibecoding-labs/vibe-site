To participate in the Vibe Coding community, you'll need a Solana wallet.

**Recommended Wallets:**

**Setup Instructions:**
1. Download your chosen wallet extension or mobile app
2. Create a new wallet and securely store your seed phrase
3. Add some SOL for transaction fees (start with ~0.1 SOL)

## ü§î Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need programming experience?**  
A: Not necessarily! We welcome all skill levels. Start with our beginner resources and grow with the community.

**Q: What programming languages should I know?**  
A: For Solana development, Rust is primary. TypeScript/JavaScript for dApps. But start where you're comfortable!

**Q: How much does it cost to join?**  
A: Basic community access is free. You'll need a small amount of SOL for blockchain transactions. Premium features may require digital passes.

**Q: How long does it take to earn my first NFT?**  
A: Depends on your pace! Some earn their first credential NFT within a week. Take your time and enjoy learning.

**Q: Can I contribute if I'm not a developer?**  
A: Absolutely! We need help with documentation, design, community management, content creation, and more.

**Q: What if I need help?**  
A: Ask in our Discord! Our community is here to support you. Check #help or #beginner-questions.

**Q: Are there any age restrictions?**  
A: Participants must be at least 13 years old. Some features may have additional requirements.

**Q: How do I stay updated?**  
A: Follow us on Twitter, join Discord, and enable notifications for announcements.
